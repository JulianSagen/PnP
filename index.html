Our solution

Loo is an application that lets you find and rent toilets publicly. The application for you, the user, and possibly the owner.

  • The application contains the following:
  • Login profile (user and owner)
  • Map and GPS navigation
  • Payment methods (Vipps, PayPal, card and cash)
  • Information about individual toilets
  • Rating system
  • Active/inactive status

User

Registration

For people who need to use the toilet we offer a simple login system by using Facebook, Google, or the user can create an entirely new account using our own account forms.

Maps

Locating your nearest toilet is simple. Just open the app, and we will locate the one closest to you. Though we have added our own functions to serve our cause better. We use simple universal icons to show the available facilities at your selected location.

Map function

By selecting the markers on the map, a window will pop up showing the address of the toilet’s location, what type of toilet it is, and other users ratings, photos of the toilet and it’s condition. If the toilet is owned by a private owner, the order button will show and you can select on it. By doing so the owner of the toilet will know that you are on your way.

Payment methods

Payment depends on the owner of the private toilet. At the moment, we offer the use of Vipps, PayPal, card and cash. You can register your with card or use PayPal to send money to the owner, or you can send money with Vipps directly in our application.

Rating system

We have set up a rating system so that you can share your toilet experience. Set stars from 1-5, take a picture and comment. The picture has to be approved by the owner of the toilet before it will be visible to the public.


Owner

If you wish to rent out your facilities

Registration

For people who wishes the to rent out their toilets and make a little something on the side, you can use the same login profile you made when you needed to locate a toilet. If you haven’t already, follow the instructions by either using our account forms or login with Facebook, Google or Microsoft.

Register toilet

To register a toilet, select owner followed by add toilet. Enter the information needed to proceed, like your full name, address, functions of toilet, bank account number etc. If you wish to conceal your bank account information, we offer the use of PayPal. Also enter if you are a business or a private home.

Active/inactive function

If you are not at home or not available for renting out your toilet, you can set your status to inactive. By doing so, your marker on the map disappears. This way no one knows of your location. To make the toilet visible again, set your status to active. Now your toilet and location are back on the map.
